# 📝 MEAL Survey Systems

## Overview

Designed and supported digital data collection systems for baseline studies, endline evaluations, routine monitoring, assessments, registrations and learning activities across multiple countries.

## Challenge

Programs required reliable, scalable and user-friendly systems capable of collecting high-quality data in low-resource environments.

## My Role

- Designed XLSForms
- Built survey logic
- Conducted testing and piloting
- Implemented quality assurance procedures
- Trained country teams and enumerators
- Provided ongoing technical support

## Technologies

- ODK
- SurveyCTO
- KoboToolbox
- ONA
- XLSForm

## Key Features Developed

### Advanced Survey Logic

- Relevance conditions
- Skip patterns
- Dynamic calculations
- Repeat groups
- Multi-level rosters

### Data Quality Controls

- Constraints
- Validation rules
- Enumerator checks
- Real-time monitoring
- Built-in error prevention mechanisms

### Multi-language Support

Developed forms supporting multiple languages and country-specific contexts.

## Impact

- Supported collection of high-quality data across multiple countries
- Reduced data entry errors
- Improved survey efficiency and user experience
- Strengthened data quality and accountability processes
